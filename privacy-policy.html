Track Race Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Track Race is designed to keep data use light. The game stores a random install identifier for leaderboard use, keeps best times on your device, and uses Google Analytics for basic usage insights.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Track Race handles information when you play the game and use leaderboard features.

Information Track Race Uses

  • Random install identifier: Track Race creates a random identifier and stores it on your device so the same installation can be recognized for leaderboard-related features.
  • Best times and leaderboard entries: If you finish a race, the game may save your best times locally and may send score data such as the event, time, identifier, and submission date to a cloud-based database used for leaderboard features.
  • Basic analytics: Track Race uses Google Analytics to understand overall usage, such as visits, session activity, engagement, and general technical details about the device or browser environment.

How This Information Is Used

  • To show personal best times and leaderboard results.
  • To keep the same installation connected to its submitted scores.
  • To understand how Track Race is used and improve performance and design over time.

Third-Party Services

Track Race relies on a small number of third-party services to support core features.

  • Cloud-based leaderboard storage: Used to store and retrieve best times linked to a random install identifier.
  • Google Analytics: Used for basic usage analytics.

These services have their own privacy policies and terms.

Storage and Control

  • The random install identifier and local best times are stored on your device.
  • Clearing app data or uninstalling the game may remove the information stored locally on your device.
  • Leaderboard entries that have already been submitted may remain on the leaderboard service unless removed there.

Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the "Last updated" date on this page will be revised.
